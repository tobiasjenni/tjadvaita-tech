Your Secret Hideaway

Haad Dao Deuk — Our Private Cove

Bounty Resort sits on the southern tip of Haad Yao beach, home to a tiny private cove with powder-white sand and spectacular sunset views. Just a 5-minute walk from Haad Yao's vibrant nightlife and restaurants.

Our resort blends cozy rooms and island-style bungalows around a scenic garden-viewpoint swimming pool. All rooms come with air conditioning, minibar, kettle, and hot shower.

Services & Facilities

Everything You Need

From coral reef snorkeling to sunset dining — we've got your island experience covered.

🤿

Snorkeling

Dive straight into the rich coral reef from Haad Dao Deuk. We also arrange sightseeing and fishing trips on traditional Thai longtail boats.

🏊

Swimming Pool

Our scenic garden-viewpoint pool overlooks the sea — the perfect spot to cool off and watch the sunset.

🏍️

Bike Rental

Explore the island at your own pace. We offer motorcycles for rent so you can discover every hidden corner of Koh Phangan.

🚐

Shuttle Service

Hassle-free transfers from the port to our resort. Just let us know your arrival time.

🍽️

Restaurant

Open-plan hillside dining with stunning sunset views. Serving delicious Thai and Western cuisine — dine traditional Thai-style or at a table.

🧺

Laundry Service

Stay fresh during your island getaway. Drop off your laundry and we'll take care of it.
